Episode I

Maximising Opportunities Along the Journey


Welcome to DreamBig.

DreamBig will help you with:

* tracking and progressing your journey through university

* learning more about your profession and possible career pathways

* considering and involving yourself in extra-curricular opportunities

* becoming truly work-ready.

DreamBig will help you have a well-balanced portfolio that makes you work-ready by the time you finish your course.

It will give you hints and tips on what you should aim for at the right time. You can also add your own targets..

Your developing professional identity will be shown as a growing star that changes as you travel through galaxies.

Each trimester you can set your goals, add activities and reflect on your achievements.
